Chhaava

IMDb Rating 8.5/10
Release Date February 14, 2025
Genre Action, Drama, History
Director Laxman Utekar
Runtime 161 min

Set in 17th-century India, Chhaava chronicles the life of Chhatrapati Sambhaji Maharaj, the valiant son of Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The film delves into Sambhaji's ascent to the throne, his fierce resistance against Mughal emperor Aurangzeb, and his unwavering commitment to preserving Maratha sovereignty. Through intense battles, political intrigue, and personal sacrifices, the narrative paints a vivid portrait of a warrior king whose legacy continues to inspire.
